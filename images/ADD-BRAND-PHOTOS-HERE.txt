SANGAM GEMSTONE — add your brand pictures
=========================================

1) LOGO (black circle with jewellery) — save as:
   sangam-logo.png
   Put this file in THIS folder (images).

2) GREEN BANNER FLYER (collage of jewellery) — save as:
   sangam-banner.png
   Same folder.

After saving, refresh your website. The home page will show the logo in the hero
and the banner below it.

To use YOUR product photos: upload to imgbb.com (or similar), copy the direct
https link, and paste it as the "image" value in products.json (or your Google Sheet).
